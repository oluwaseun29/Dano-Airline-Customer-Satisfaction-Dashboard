# Dano-Airline-Customer-Satisfaction-Dashboard

INTRODUCTION

This project presents a data-driven analysis of customer satisfaction for Dano Airlines, a UK-based airline headquartered in London. The analysis was conducted in response to a decline in customer satisfaction, which fell below 50% for the first time. The objective is to identify the key factors influencing passenger satisfaction and provide actionable recommendations to improve the overall customer experience.
Using a dataset containing over 120,000 passenger survey responses, this project explores customer demographics, travel characteristics, flight information, service quality ratings, and satisfaction levels.
 The analysis focuses on uncovering patterns and trends that help explain customer perceptions 
and highlight opportunities for service improvement.
An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to visualize key performance indicators (KPIs), 
customer segments, service ratings, and the impact of factors such as travel class, customer type, and flight delays on passenger satisfaction.

RECOMMENDATION 
Based on the dashboard analysis, the following actions are recommended to improve customer satisfaction at Dano Airlines:
1.Improve the Economy Class Experience 
1.The overall satisfaction rate is only 43.44%, indicating that many passengers are dissatisfied. 
Dano Airlines should focus on improving seat comfort, legroom, in-flight entertainment, and meal quality, particularly for Economy Class passengers. 
2.Reduce Flight Delays 

1.The dashboard shows that customer satisfaction is highest for flights with no delays, while satisfaction 
declines as delays increase. The airline should improve operational planni
ng, aircraft scheduling, and communication with passengers during disruptions.


CONCLUSION 

The analysis of 130,000 passenger records reveals that Dano Airlines currently has an overall customer satisfaction rate of 43.44%, with 73,000 dissatisfied customers compared to 56,000 satisfied customers. The findings indicate that flight delays, customer type, and travel characteristics have a significant influence on passenger satisfaction.
To improve customer experience, Dano Airlines should prioritize reducing operational delays, enhancing onboard services, strengthening customer loyalty programs, and addressing the needs of customer groups with the highest dissatisfaction. By implementing these recommendations and continuously monitoring customer feedback, the airline can improve passenger satisfaction, increase customer retention, and strengthen its competitive position in the aviation industry.
